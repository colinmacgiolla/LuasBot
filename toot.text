St. Patrick’s Day 17 March Service InfoOn St. 
Patrick’s Day Sunday 17 March, the Luas Red and Green Lines will run Sunday Operating Hours. To facilitate the St. Patrick’s Day Parade, the following temporary partial closures of both Lines will be in place: Red Line: Red Lie services are running normal Sunday Operating Hours Apologies for any inconvenience caused. 
Green Line: From 07:00 – 18:00 there will be no trams running between St. 
Stephen’s Green and Dominick. During this time, trams will run between Broombridge and Dominick, and between St. 
Stephen’s Green and Brides Glen. Trams will run normal Sunday Operating Hours on Bank Holiday Monday 18 March. 
All lifts and escalators are in full working order.
