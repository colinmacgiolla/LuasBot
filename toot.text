Red Line Services Operating With Delays. Green Line Services Operating Normally. Service Update: There are significant delays on the Luas Red Line this evening due to a tram losing power at Heuston Stop. The affected tram has now been moved to the outbound track and will be towed back to the Red Cow depot, which is expected to take at least 30 minutes. Normal inbound services to the City Centre have resumed. Luas tickets are valid on Dublin Bus during this disruption. We apologise for the inconvenience and appreciate your patience. 
All Green Line services are operating normally. 
The Northbound and Southbound lift at Phibsborough, the Charlemont lift, and the trackside escalator at Connolly are currently out of service. We apologise for any inconvenience caused. 
All other lifts and escalators are in working order.
