Due to an operational issue at Red Cow, Red Line services are currently not operating from Red Cow to Tallaght. Trams are running from Tallaght to city centre and between Belgard and Saggart. Dublin Bus will accept all valid tickets for the duration of this disruption. We apologise for any inconvenience caused. 
All Green Line services are operating normally. 
The descending and ascending escalator and lift at Connolly and the Northbound lift at Kilmacud are currently out of service. We apologise for any inconvenience caused. 
All other lifts and escalators are in working order.
