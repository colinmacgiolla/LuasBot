Due to a failed tram Red Line services are currently not operating between Blackhorse and Belgard. Trams are operating between Tallaght/Saggart and Belgard and between Blackhorse and The Point. Dublin Bus will accept all valid tickets for the duration of this disruption. We apologise for any inconvenience caused. 
All Green Line services are operating normally. 
All lifts and escalators are in full working order. 
The Cabra Road lift or the Southbound (inbound) Lift at Phibsborough will be taken out of service on Tuesday 4th of June and returned to service, on or before, Wednesday June 12th. 
The second lift, the North circular Road lift or the Northbound (outbound) Lift, at Phibsborough will be taken out of service on Thursday 13th of June and returned to service, on or before, Friday June 21st.
