Passengers,Please be advised Red Line services are operating between Tallaght / Saggart to Smithfield only. There is no service between Smithfield - The Point / Connolly due to an issue at Jervis. Luas tickets are valid on Dublin Bus for the duration of this disruption. We apologise for any inconvenience caused. 
All Green Line services are operating normally. 
The lift at Kilmacud Inbound and the ascending escalator at Connolly are out of service. We apologise for any inconvenience caused. 
All other lifts and escalators are in full working order. Red Line Services Due to Luas engineering works taking place on the Red Line this weekend, Luas Stops between Blackhorse and Heuston will be closed on Sat 30 Sept and Sun 1 Oct. Trams will run between Tallaght / Saggart and Blackhorse, and between Heuston and Connolly / The Point only. Replacement buses will run approx. every 15 mins in the affected area with some restrictions due to limited bus set down areas. Please see https://luas. ie/red-line-works-Sepoct23 for information. 
Green Line services will not be affected by these works. Thank you for your patience and cooperation during these works.
