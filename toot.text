Red Line Service Info: Saturday 2 & Sunday 3 SeptemberRed Line Services Due to Luas engineering works taking place on the Red Line this weekend, Luas Stops between Blackhorse and Heuston will be closed on Sat 2 and Sun 3 Sept. Replacement buses will run approx. every 15 mins in the affected area with some restrictions due to limited bus set down areas. Please see https://luas. ie/red-line-works-Sept23 for information.
