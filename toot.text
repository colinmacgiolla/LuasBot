Due to a car stuck on the tracks at Kingswood Red Line services are currently not operating between Red Cow and Belgard. Trams are running between Red Cow and The Point and between Saggart/Tallaght and Belgard. Dublin Bus will accept all valid tickets for the duration of this disruption. We apologise for any inconvenience caused. 
All Green Line services are operating normally. 
The lifts at Dundrum and Connolly are out of service. We apologise for any inconvenience caused. 
All other lifts and escalators are in full working order. Passengers travelling to Leopardstown Racecourse, alight at Sandyford for the free shuttle bus service. 
Advance Notice: Irish Olympic Teams Homecoming There will be a change to Luas Red and Green Line Services on Monday August 12 2024, to allow us to celebrate the Irish Olympic teams homecoming on O'Connell Street. Please check the Luas Website, App and Social Media accounts and listen to traffic and travel updates for more detailed information closer to the day.
