There is no Green Line Service this morning due to a technical issue with the overhead power lines. Luas technicians are on site and working to resolve the matter. Tickets are valid on Dublin Bus. Red Line Services are operating normally. 
Detailed update on the Green Line. 
Customers, this morning, Luas Drivers identified an issue with the  Green Line overhead power line which has resulted in a total suspension of service. Luas technicians are currently carrying out inspections on the overhead line and all tram pantographs. These inspections will take a number of hours to complete. We apologise for the inconvenience. 
Once complete; the Green line will open up in phases. 
The first phase will be between Brides Glen and St Stephen’s Green. 
The second phase will be St Stephen’s Green to Broombridge. At all times your ticket will be valid on Dublin Bus. Staff have been deployed to key Luas stops to assist customers and we encourage customersto check the Luas Web and app and listen to traffic and travel updates. 
All lifts and escalators are in full working order.
